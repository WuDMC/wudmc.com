Visionz would be a cutting-edge, data-driven platform designed to automate and optimize up to 90% of the job search process.

Still in development, here are the planned features:

  • ⊚ All Jobs in one place: Scraping from Telegram, LinkedIn, Facebook, job boards, and corporate resources.
  • ⊚ Job tracker: Start work with job offers in 1-click and easy track the progress.
  • ⊚ Data Insights Interactive dashboards displaying where and when jobs were posted.

  • ⊚ Easy-Apply: Automated and manual applying options.
  • ⊚ Learning path: ML&AI-recommendations based on resume and job comparison.
  • ⊚ Salary Insights : View salary estimates for different positions and industries.
  • ⊚ Interview Preparation: Access tips and resources to help prepare for interviews.

Stay tuned for more updates as Visionz continues to evolve and make job searching smarter, faster, and more personalized!
